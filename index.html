Our Story

More Than a Meal —
A Taste of Home

At Sabor a México, we believe the best Mexican food is made with heart. Nestled inside Meadows Mall, we bring the warmth of authentic Mexican cooking straight to Las Vegas.

Every dish is crafted from time-honored recipes — vibrant salsas, slow-seasoned meats, and handmade tortillas that fill the air with the kind of aroma that makes you feel welcome before you even sit down.

Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch, enjoying a family dinner, or picking up takeaway, we're here to make every meal a celebration.
